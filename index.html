A young company built on old-school work ethic

JonLexx Construction was founded by Jonatan Garcia, who started learning the trades at twelve years old and launched the company at twenty-three to provide for his growing family. What began as one man's determination has grown into a crew East Texas homeowners and businesses trust with their biggest projects.

We do not just build on site. We plan, we communicate, and we leave every project cleaner than we found it. Quality is a choice, and we make it on every job.
