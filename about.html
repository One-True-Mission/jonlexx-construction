Our Story

Started at twelve. Founded at twenty-three.

Jonatan Garcia started learning the trades at twelve years old. By twenty-three, with his first child on the way and a family to provide for, he took everything he had learned and put it to work under his own name. JonLexx Construction was born from that decision.

Two consistent years of study and hands-on work built the business into what it is today. We will be the first to tell you we are still growing. What we will not do is let scale come before standards. Every project gets better materials, better planning, and better execution, because quality is a choice and we choose it every time.

We aim to serve and serve to bless. When you hire JonLexx, you are not a ticket number. You are the reason the company exists.

  • Family-founded and family-motivated
  • Trades experience that started long before the LLC
  • A growing crew with a fixed standard
