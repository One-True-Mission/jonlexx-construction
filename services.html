What We Do

Concrete Work

Slabs, driveways, sidewalks, patios, and shop floors. We handle the forming, the pour, and the finish, and we leave the site extremely clean when the concrete is down. Whether you need a 20x20 shop slab or a full driveway replacement, concrete is the work JonLexx built its reputation on.

Drainage, Landscape & Retaining Walls

Standing water and washed-out yards do not fix themselves. We correct grading, reroute gutter drainage, and build retaining walls that hold. Solutions are engineered for East Texas soil and East Texas storms, so the fix lasts.

Shower Remodel

From dated fiberglass inserts to custom tile showers built watertight from the studs out. We handle demo, waterproofing, tile, glass, and fixtures, and we keep the rest of your home clean while we work.

Bathtub Remodel

Tub swaps, tub-to-shower conversions, and full surround rebuilds. We help you pick fixtures and finishes that fit your budget, then install them right the first time.

Full Renovation, Exterior & Interior

Whole-room and whole-home renovations handled by one accountable crew. Framing, drywall, flooring, paint, trim, siding, and everything between, coordinated so your project moves instead of stalling between subs.

Home Building, Shops & Metal Buildings

Ground-up builds for homes, workshops, barns, and metal buildings. We take projects from site prep and slab through framing and finish-out, with one point of contact from the first walkthrough to the last.
